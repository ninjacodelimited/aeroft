Welcome To AeroFT

AeroFT (Aerospace Future Technologies), is a UK-based startup that exists to develop novel aerospace products which will directly or indirectly improve the productivity, quality and sustainability of the global population and eco system. We are redefining the fixed wing UAS platform and incorporating our simple, yet effective take on VTOL capabilities.

Efficient, cost-effective and a whole lot cooler.

AEROFT

About Us

Spawned from a combination of academic research and a hobby interest, the decision to go forward with a new UAS concept was made. We worked on various designs and methods to develop a concept that is unique and features solutions to boost the capability of a fixed wing platform system.
Our team of engineers have combined their experience from a variety of backgrounds and industries to turn our concept into a package that fits the demands of the commercial operator.

Reinventing capabilities in autonomous flying systems

Moving forward from the standard is how technology and society progresses. We are taking steps to show that the fixed-wing UAS platform can take on a cool and better performing look!

Doing something new and fun

Developing products is not just about finding new things to sell. It’s also about further developing our technical understanding and help inject new ideas into the aerospace and engineering community.

Improvements in aerodynamics and structures

There are still more tricks up the sleeve when it comes to aerodynamics and optimising for more endurance and range.

Clever use of materials and techniques

Making use of current state of the art processes, including 3D printing and low volume manufacturing techniques is a good way to mix things up! We save on significant costs without hindering structural mass and overall performance.
