AeroFT

AeroFT is a privately founded U.K based company that exists to develop and realise novel aerospace ideas, which transpire into practical solutions to serve current real world challenges.

We are passionate about developing commercially viable solutions, combining

  • 3D printing technology
  • Renewable energy systems
  • Recyclable Materials
  • Well established production lines and manufacturing practices

to assist in the expansion of civil UAVs for practical and ethical applications.

We also aim to open up new niche markets as well as develop new research areas that allow for the cross over of various engineering disciplines for aerospace systems.

Our Vision

To be recognised as an innovation think tank who can deliver practical solutions within the growing market of civil autonomous systems and future aerospace technologies and initiatives.

The Skyhawk

We are developing an innovative hybrid structured Unmanned Aerial Vehicle (UAV) capable of a variety of long endurance civil mission roles. Late in 2014, we examined and developed a concept that utilises a flexible fabric structure integrated within a low cost and low mass rigid airframe. The result of our work is the AeroFT Skyhawk. We are proud to apply current 100% renewable technology in a unique fashion that allows us to meet the need of long endurance flight for small to medium-sized systems.

Timeline

  • 2015: AeroFT founded
  • April 2015: Skyhawk detailed design optimisation
